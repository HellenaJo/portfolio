Ugandan rolex

Rolex is a popular food item in Uganda, combining an egg omelette,veggies and meat or chicken wrapped in a chapati. This single-portion dish is quick to prepare, and can be eaten at any time of the day, from breakfast to a lunch or supper meal or snack. The name "rolex" comes from its method of preparation, with the chapati and the omelette rolled together ("rolled eggs"). When talking about mouth-watering food, the rolex tops the charts.
